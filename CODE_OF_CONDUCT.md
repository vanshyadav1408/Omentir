# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Participation in this project must be harassment-free for everyone. Be respectful, assume good intent, accept constructive feedback, and keep disagreements focused on ideas and evidence.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, publishing private information, or sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `vanshyadav.1408@gmail.com`. The project owner may remove content or restrict participation when necessary.
